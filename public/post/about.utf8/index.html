rCITI: travel survey

Project description

You are invited to participate in this research study. The aim of this research study is to collect information about the personal travel behaviour of residents of the Sydney metropolitan area. Information about people’s day-to-day travel behaviour is critical to predict future travel demand and infrastructure needs.

Requirements:

  • You are eighteen years old or older
  • You reside in the metropolitan area of Sydney, Australia.
  • You own a smartphone running Android (compatibility varies with device) or Apple iOS 9 (or newer).
  • You have a Gmail account*.
  • if you are not comfortable sharing your gmail address with us, you can create a fake account just for this study.
